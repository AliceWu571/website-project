Genre: Drama / Sports
Producer Country/Region: China / Hong Kong, China
Release Date: 2020-09-25(Mainland China)

IMDb: tt10670442

Plot summary
　　August 15, 2008, Beijing Olympics, China vs USA. Lang Ping (Gong Li), wearing gold-rimmed glasses, sits in the USA coach's chair, calm and collected, her gaze electric; the Chinese coach (Huang Bo) stands on the sidelines, attentive and smiling. The coach of the Chinese team looks at Lang Ping, his gaze full of deep meaning. The constant passing of people obscures his vision, and the picture of more than thirty years of Chinese women's volleyball is slowly opened ......
